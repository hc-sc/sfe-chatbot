New Cellular Phone/Service

Device and request details

Helpful information for Device and request details

Use these services to:

  • Request a new mobile phone if you currently don't have a mobile phone.
    • Smart phone with data and email:
      • Android (Samsung)
      • iOS (iPhone)
    • Cellular phone with voice and text only
  • Replace mobile phone if you have a broken or defective device. The phone number can be transferred to the new device via number portability or a swap.
  • Transfer mobile phone from another department you are coming from another department and would like to keep current mobile phone.
  • Reactivate mobile phone if you are coming from extended leave and the mobile phone if it was disconected

Use Additional information text box to provide business justification or further clarify requirements.

Helpful information for Approver information

Provide complete name, approval level and email address of person who is approving the request. An email will be sent to this individual after the order is processed.

Director approval is required for:

  • New cellular phone
  • Replacing smart phone
  • Reactivating cellular phone

DG approval is required for:

  • New smart phone
  • Reactivating smart phone
