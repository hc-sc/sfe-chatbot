Hardware

Hardware includes any IT equipment and accessories HC / PHAC employees need to do their work:

  • IT equipment

    Primary IT equipment

    Primary IT equipment is equipment you were provided with upon your arrival at HC / PHAC. It includes basic IT tools you need to do your every day job:

    • Computer (laptop)
    • Monitor
    • Keyboard
    • Mouse
    • Lock
    • Docking station

    Additional IT equipment

    Additional IT equipment is equipment that you might require in addition to the primary equipment to perform the work.

    Additional IT equipment includes, but is not limited to:

    • Non-standard computer (exceeds the IMSD standard, i.e. faster processor)
    • Non-standard single monitor (exceeds the IMSD standard, i.e. larger size)
    • Additional computer
    • Additional monitor
    • Travel laptop
    • Portable storage devices
      • Secure USB
      • External hard drives
      • eSATA devices
    • Tablet
    • Scanner
    • Satellite phone

  • Printers

    Network and local printers

    A network printer is a device on the HC / PHAC network that can support the printing needs of multiple users at the same time.

    A local printer directly connects to a specific computer and it is only that computer that can access or be serviced by the printer.

  • Accessories

    Accessories are smaller products that provide additional functions to your IT equipment or enhance usability and add protection. Accessories include, but are not limited to:

    • Accessory devices
      • Special keyboard - ergonomic, wireless, unilingual
      • Special mouse - ergonomic, wireless
      • Speakers / headphones
      • Label maker
      • Other
    • Other small accessories
      • Mouse pad
      • Tablet cover
      • Laptop bag
      • Other

This service supports hardware acquisition, installation, support and disposal in accordance with HC / PHAC technology standards.
