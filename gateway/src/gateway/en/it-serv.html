Branch IT services

IT enabled business solutions

Many organizations are increasingly turning their attention to opportunities to achieve a competitive advantage through information technology solutions. Rapid technological change is shaping and reshaping the business world as we know it and while this presents challenges, it also exposes new opportunities for organizations to improve their value-added offerings to their clients. In IMSD, we fully understand the trends impacting the Government and the importance of smart coordination of the technology that supports corporate strategies.

New requirements

IMSD strives to proactively suggest new solutions or enhancements to existing IM/IT solutions, based on client needs and business requirements. IMSD will work collaboratively with  clients to identify business challenges and requirements, with which IM/IT options and solutions can be developed, configured and modified

We provide:

  • custom software development
  • configuration and customization of COTS products
  • mobile application development/web application development and web standards (includes web accessibility assessments and testing of web and mobile applications and PDF forms based on TBS Web Standards)
  • application maintenance, data management and analytics (database administration/extract, transform and load/SAS environments/Cognos environments and reporting services)
  • solutions architecture (options analysis, application architecture, technology architecture), and
  • technology portfolio management (stewardship of technologies used in HC / PHAC).

IT security assessments (e.g. SOS, TRA, SAAR, vulnerability assessment)

IT security assessments are required and designed to prevent security disruptions and risks for individual users and to the HC and PHAC IT infrastructure.

Statement of Sensitivity (SOS):
Is a process geared towards determining the level of sensitivity of the information within the system. This is comprised of three processes (asset identification, injury assessment, asset valuation).
Threat and Risk Assessment (TRA):
Is a process focused on identifying system assets, threats to those assets, and vulnerabilities which would allow threats to compromise the assets. The process assesses the level of risk posed to the assets and recommends how to manage the risks, including safeguards to mitigate risks to acceptable levels.
Security Assessment and Authorization Report (SAAR):
Is the process of evaluating security controls to establish the extent to which the controls are implemented correctly, operating as intended, and achieving the desired outcome with respect to meeting security requirements. Security assessments support security authorization. This is completed with a security risk management decision and to explicitly accept the related residual risk, based on the results of security assessment and monitoring.
Vulnerability Assessment:
Is a series of scans conducted to identify vulnerabilities to existing applications and then provides recommendations to patch/fix these underlying issues.

  • determining, based on technology considerations, which business applications are at risk

IT security advice and guidance

Disruptions to network security, both intentional (e.g. hacking) and accidental (e.g. clicking a malicious link in phishing email), compromise the integrity of IM/IT service delivery and have far reaching business impacts for the Department and Agency. IT Security offers services in support of solution enablement, development and maintenance to prevent and mitigate these issues. This approach to IT Security is designed to mitigate risks, support IT continuity planning, support unique business needs and ensure that clients are working with a safe and secure IT environment.
