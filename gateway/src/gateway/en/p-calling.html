New calling or service feature

Use this service to get new calling or service feature for cell/smart, BlackBerry and landline phones.

Before requesting new calling or service feature get approvals from your director. You will be asked to attach approved email message to your request.

New calling or service feature request

Note

If you are from PMRA branch, please contact PMRA facilities at 613-736-3422 to resolve your landline-related issues and requests.

  • download and complete SSC Mobile device request and approval form (PDF)

You will be asked to attach the completed form to your request.
