Phone and internet

This service provides HC / PHAC employees with devices and services to support their communications requirements. It includes mobile and landline phones and services, wireless access to internet and network (Wi-Fi), as well as the peripheral equipment necessary for HC / PHAC users to communicate with each other via phone or internet.

Mobile phones

  • Cellular phone with voice and text only
  • Smart phone with data and email:
    • Android (Samsung)
    • iOS (iPhone)

Landline

Traditional desktop phone or service that is physically attached to a phone jack with wires.
