Cancel mobile phone

Use these services to cancel or suspend your mobile phone if you are:

  • returning a mobile phone that is no longer needed and closing the account
  • returning a mobile phone that has been replaced, and
  • transferring a mobile phone for an employee that is moving to a different Government department

The services apply to:

  • Smart phone with data and email:
    • Android (Samsung)
    • iOS (iPhone)
  • Cellular phone with voice and text only

Before cancelling cellular or smart phone or service:

  • get branch director approval, and
  • download and complete SSC Mobile device request and approval form (PDF)

You will be asked to attach the completed form to your request.

Cancel cellular phone request Cancel smart phone request

Note

After request to cancel the phone is approved, package your unwanted Cellphone or other cellular device and forward through internal mail to SSC Wireless Services. Include a hard copy of your ticket to facilitate processing within SSC.
