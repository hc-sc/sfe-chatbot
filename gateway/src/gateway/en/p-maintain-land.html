Maintain landline phone lines

If you are experiencing problems with a landline phone line, you can submit a request for support.

Landline

Traditional desktop phone or service that is physically attached to a phone jack with wires.

Before submitting a support request, you can help expedite resolution by executing a few simple steps to determine if the problem is related to the phone line or the telephone (desktop or polycom).

  • Ensure the correct wall jack for the phone line is being used.
  • Ensure the telephone wire (between the wall jack and the telephone) is securely plugged in at both ends.Make a call from/to this phone line. Is the problem resolved?
  • Replace the telephone with another (well functioning) telephone. Make a call from/to this phone line. Is the problem resolved by replacing the telephone?
  • Plug the original telephone into another (well functioning) phone line. Has the problem transferred to the new phone line?

If the problem is related to the telephone (problem is resolved by using a new telephone on the original phone line, or moves to a new phone line when using the original telephone) then you will want to replace the telephone set.

Replacing Polycom conference (speaker) phones is a branch responsibility (client funded), purchased directly through a regular supply order. For other desktop telephone equipment, refer to New landline phone and service.

If the problem persists on the phone line, proceed to submit a request for landline phone lines support and be sure to provide your results from the trouble shooting steps above.
