Get printers

HC / PHAC users have access to a large variety of multifunction network printers with scanner, photocopier and fax capabilities as well as local printers .

Network printer

A network printer is a device on the HC / PHAC network that can support the printing needs of multiple users at the same time.

Local printer

A local printer directly connects to a specific computer and it is only that computer that can access or be serviced by the printer.

Request new printer (network or local)

You will be asked to choose a device based on it’s specifications. If the specifications do not meet your requirements, you have the option of following the SAP non-catalogue process. Requests for hardware items through the SAP non-catalogue process require more time to process as testing and approvals must be completed prior to installation on the HC / PHAC network.

New printer request

Note

  • All network printers in HC / PHAC are leased devices and must be certified models that have been approved by IT Security. Each client area will continue to be responsible for the cost of these devices, including consumables, maintenance and repair.
  • For amendments to existing printer leases, contact the Procurement and Contracting Unit at hc.contracts.east-est.contrats.sc@canada.ca or refer to the Procurement and Contracting page on mySOURCE to assist you with your request.

SAP account

  • All product requests are done through SAP business application. If you don’t have an account with SAP, please contact your CFOB SAP branch security officer.

For immediate or urgent assistance, please call the National Service Desk at 613-954-8718 (NCR) or 1-800-416-0358 (regions)
