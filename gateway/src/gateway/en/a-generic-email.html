Functional (generic) email accounts

A functional (generic) email account can be accessed by multiple users to read and send emails and has a unique email address and mailbox. It is created and used as a contact method for a specific business service, function or group. This ensures a consistent contact point, which does not change when its users change roles or leave. It also ensures that a group of users can access the generic mailbox to retrieve and action emails, reducing the risk of emails remaining unattended in an individual’s mailbox.

Create, rename or delete functional (generic) email account

To create new functional (generic) email account, rename or delete existing, a request must be placed. If you are not the account owner, but need an account be renamed or deleted, you must get approval from the account owner before placing your request.
