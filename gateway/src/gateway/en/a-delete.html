Delete an account

Before deleting any account, you must first give due consideration to your Information Management responsibilities. Determine if the account you want to delete contains any information that is or may be, part of any active or pending Access to Information (ATI) requests or legal matters.

  • If so:
    1. Preserve the account and the information exactly "as is".
    2. Contact hc.im.info-info.gi.sc@canada.ca or your Branch Information Management Advisor (BIMA) for further instructions.
  • If not:
    1. Identify and move all content that qualifies as Information Resources of Business Value (IRBV) to an official corporate information repository (e.g. shared drives, GC docs, RDIMS).

Once your Information Management responsibilities have been addressed, you may then initiate account deletion by completing the "IT Account Deletion Form".

IT Account Deletion Form

Note

  • All requests to delete IT accounts must be placed a minimum of 5 days in advance of the desired implementation date.
  • If you require assistance with any aspect of Information Management responsibilities, your Branch Information Management Advisor (BIMA) or your manager can assist you.

Employee / Non-employee Departure Process

  • As the "IT Account Deletion Form" is part of the "Employee / Non-employee Departure Process", you will be automatically directed to complete the departure process.
  • Completion of the departure process is required when staff leave HC / PHAC, whether the leave is permanent (e.g. deployment, retirement) or temporary and lasting greater than six months (e.g. parental leave, secondment).
  • The departure process includes (in addition to account deletion) opportunity to request pick-up of IT equipment for return / recycle / disposal.

For immediate or urgent assistance, please call the National Service Desk at 613-954-8718 (NCR) or 1-800-416-0358 (regions)
