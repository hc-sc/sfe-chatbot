Support for IT equipment and accessories

If your computing device or other IT equipment is not working properly, meeting your needs or performing to your expectations, support services are available. We will troubleshoot to determine the level of repair that is required or resolve, whenever possible, the IT issues that you are having.

Note

If you need assistance to move IT equipment and / or other office contents to a new office, visit Office relocation page.

Primary IT equipment

Full support and maintenance is provided to all standard IT equipment regardless of the level of repair required.

IT primary equipment includes:

  • Computer (e.g. desktop, laptop)
  • Monitor
  • Keyboard
  • Mouse
  • Lock (laptop)
  • Docking station (laptop)
Support for primary IT equipment request
Additional IT equipment

Support is also available for, but is not limited to:

  • Non-standard computer (exceeds the IMSD standard, i.e. faster processor)
  • Non-standard single monitor (exceeds the IMSD standard, i.e. larger size)
  • Additional computer
  • Additional monitor
  • Travel laptop
  • Portable storage devices
    • Secure USB
    • External hard drives
    • eSATA devices
  • Tablet
  • Scanner
  • Satellite phone
Support for additional IT equipment request
Accessory devices

Support is available for accessory devices including, but not limited to:

  • Special keyboard - ergonomic, wireless, unilingual
  • Special mouse - ergonomic, wireless
  • Speakers / headphones
  • Label maker
  • Other
Support for accessory devices request

For immediate or urgent assistance, please call the National Service Desk at 613-954-8718 (NCR) or 1-800-416-0358 (regions)
