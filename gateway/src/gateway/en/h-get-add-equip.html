Get IT equipment

Upon arrival to HC / PHAC, you are provided with your primary IT equipment through New or returning user set-up but you can request additional equipment if it is necessary to do your job.

Computers and other IT equipment

Computers and other IT equipment may include but is not limited to:

  • Non-standard computer (exceeds the IMSD standard, i.e. faster processor)
  • Non-standard single monitor (exceeds the IMSD standard, i.e. larger size)
  • Additional computer
  • Additional monitor
  • Smart board
  • Tablet
  • Scanner
  • Satellite phone

You will be asked to choose a device based on it’s specifications. If the specifications do not meet your requirements, you have the option of following the SAP non-catalogue process. Requests for hardware items through the SAP non-catalogue process require more time to process as testing and approvals must be completed prior to installation on the HC / PHAC network.

New equipment request

SAP account

  • All requests for IT equipment must be made through the SAP business application. If you don’t have an SAP account, please contact your CFOB SAP branch security officer for assistance.
Portable storage devices

A portable storage device (PSD) is an easily transportable tool that can be used to store information. PSDs are to be used for temporary storage of business information only.

Examples of PSDs include:

  • USB memory stick
  • External hard drive
  • eSATA
  • SD card
  • IT media such as CDs and DVDs
  • Any other device that has internal memory:
    • iPod
    • MP3 player
    • Camera
    • Audio recorder
    • Scanner

Getting PSD is a two step process:

  • Download and complete PSD procurement questionnaire.
  • Submit your request for a portable storage device (PSD) and upload the completed questionnaire when prompted. The information that you provided in the questionnaire will be reviewed and you will be contacted, by email, with the next steps.
Portable storage device request

Secure use

  • Only encrypted PSDs that have been approved and received through this PSD request process may be connected to your HC / PHAC computer or device.

Travel laptop

Travel laptop is provided temporarily for use for high-risk destinations or events. The laptop is not to be connected to the HC / PHAC network and will need to be wiped once returned.

Contact Security management division (SMD) at hc.security.travel.briefing-info.sur.la.securite.des.voyages.sc@canada.ca to determine if:

  • the destinations / events on your travel itinerary are considered to be low risk or high risk; and
  • additional security measures are required for your assignment.
