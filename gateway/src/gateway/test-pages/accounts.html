IT accounts & access

HC / PHAC staff need IT user accounts and access to the Government of Canada IT network infrastructure to fulfill their job functions.

In this section, requests can be made to create new and modify existing accounts and access for Outlook email, Lotus Notes accounts and Network/common drive / shared folder access. Several of the services, when requested, are designed to address the modification to all of a user's "Primary accounts" (e.g., Outlook email, Network, Lotus Notes accounts), while others will address access needs or account requests for a specific tool.

Manage passwords

Change, reset, unlock and troubleshoot your passwords for Outlook, Windows computer/workstation, Lotus Notes, laptops and mobile devices.

  • Change or reset password
  • Unlock account/password

Network accounts

Manage network, Outlook email and Lotus Notes accounts.

  • New or returning user set-up
  • Move an account
  • Delete an account
  • Extend an account
  • Change your user name

Outlook email accounts and lists

Create, rename or delete Outlook email distribution lists and functional (generic) email accounts, manage list membership and access, and get access to another user email account if required.

  • Email distribution lists
  • Functional (generic) email accounts
  • Access to another user email account

Shared drives and common folders

Get support in creating a new or modifying an existing shared drive or common folder.

  • Shared drives and common folders

myKey

Obtain your myKEY if you don’t have it, or get support if you are experiencing issues accessing applications that require myKEY.

  • myKEY

Accounts support

Report an issue with a primary network account, email account, functional (generic) email account, email distribution list, business account or other account type.

  • Accounts support

For immediate or urgent assistance, please call the National Service Desk at 613-954-8718 (NCR) or 1-800-416-0358 (regions)
